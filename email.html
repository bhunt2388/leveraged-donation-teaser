How It Works

The 5:1 Leverage Explained in Plain English

Your client acquires a partnership interest in a series holding volcanic mineral assets at a fraction of appraised fair market value. Because of IRS “tacking” rules, they inherit the long-term holding period immediately.

When the minerals are donated to the qualified charity, the deduction is taken at full appraised fair market value — not the lower cash cost. That gap is where the 5:1 ratio lives. The charity receives a real, usable, commercially valuable asset. The client receives a verified deduction. And you receive a referral fee.
